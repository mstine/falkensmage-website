**Front-matter contract (must match what Plan 06-01's `work/single.html` reads).** Each offer page front matter provides:
- `title` — the offer's short name (The Query / The Cast / The Daemon)
- `offer_name` — the plain-language what-it-is (e.g. "Archetypal Tarot Reading")
- `price` — canonical price string (`$250` / `$325` / `$4,500`)
- `duration` — `90 minutes` for the two readings; OMIT for The Daemon (6-month engagement, not a session)
- `cta_text` — exact CTA label (see canonical table)
- `booking_key` — one of `the-query` / `the-cast` / `the-daemon`, matching a key in `hugo.toml [params.booking]`
- `summary` — one-line card blurb the hub surfaces
- `weight` — controls hub ordering (10/20/30 → Query, Cast, Daemon)

The markdown BODY is the voiced pitch, rendered by `{{ .Content }}` in the pitch section. Price/duration/CTA come from front matter (the template renders them) — you may reference the transformation in prose, but the structured price/shape/CTA are front-matter fields, not body copy.

**Canonical offers (do NOT alter price/shape/CTA — 06-CONTEXT.md):**

| File | title | offer_name | price | duration | cta_text | booking_key |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| the-query.md | The Query | Archetypal Tarot Reading | $250 | 90 minutes | Book & Pay | the-query |
| the-cast.md | The Cast | Archetypal Astrology Reading | $325 | 90 minutes | Book & Pay | the-cast |
| the-daemon.md | The Daemon | 6-Month Hermetic Coaching | $4,500 | (omit) | Book a Clarity Consult ($150, credited) | the-daemon |

The Daemon's CTA is **consult-gated**: it books a $150 clarity consult credited toward the $4,500 engagement — NOT a direct $4,500 buy. Make the consult-gate legible in the pitch body ("we start with a $150 clarity consult; if we go forward it's credited").

**Voice (match `content/_index.md`).** Direct, technomagickal, lightly profane. The homepage register: "one technomagickal motherfucker," "if that made you flinch, we're probably not a fit," "Stay feral, folks." Each pitch sells the transformation first; the mechanics (price/duration) are a detail inside the pitch. Practitioner framing is **archetypal tarotist** and **chaos witch** — never "tarot reader" / "chaos magician" (project-memory rule).

**Hub framing (`content/work/_index.md`).** Needs `title` (e.g. "Work With Me") and a SHORT body — a front door to the offers, not a services essay (per 06-CONTEXT.md "Specific Ideas"). Two to four sentences of framing, then the template renders the three cards from the offer pages' front matter. No card markup in the body — the template (`work/list.html`) generates cards by ranging `.Pages`.

**Hugo section behavior.** `content/work/_index.md` becomes the section list page (renders via `work/list.html`); the three `the-*.md` regular pages render via `work/single.html`. URLs: `/work/`, `/work/the-query/`, `/work/the-cast/`, `/work/the-daemon/`.
