    **Responsive notes (THEME-05):**
    - Mobile-first: default styles target 375px+
    - `padding: 3rem 1.25rem` = 20px horizontal padding at 375px (leaves 335px content width)
    - `max-width: 40rem` at mobile prevents line lengths from getting too long
    - Breakpoints at 48rem (768px tablet) and 64rem (1024px desktop)
    - No horizontal overflow at any viewport

    **Sigil notes (D-12):**
    - `.sigil-arc::after` uses `border-radius: 50%` with 2 sides transparent = broken half-circle
    - `.sigil-arc-sm::before` is a smaller variant with different rotation for variety
    - `.sigil-gradient::after` is a void-to-glow line for section dividers
    - All use `pointer-events: none` and low opacity
    - Positioning (top/left/right/bottom) is left to the section partials in Phase 2 -- these classes define the shape, not the placement
